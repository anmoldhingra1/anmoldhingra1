### What I'm Building

**[Rerato](https://www.rerato.com)** is an AI App Factory — a platform for building AI-native apps that feel alive, not lifeless.

**[Trivana.ai](https://trivana.ai)** is our first product — personality-driven AI digital hosts for live experiences.

I believe most AI apps fail because they have zero personality. We're fixing that.
